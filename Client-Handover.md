## 3. Managing Your Store

Your Admin Dashboard is your control center. Here is what you can do:

### 🛍️ Products
- **Add New Products:** Upload images, set prices, and write descriptions for new crochet items.
- **Categories:** Organize your items into sections like *Bags, Flowers, Tops, Skirts,* and *Accessories*.
- **Edit/Delete:** Easily update stock status or fix typos in existing products.

### 📝 Reviews
- Customer reviews are crucial for building trust. When a customer submits a review, it stays hidden until you approve it.
- Navigate to the **"Reviews"** tab to read, approve, or delete incoming feedback. Approved reviews will automatically appear on your Home page.

### ⚙️ Admin Settings
- Update your Display Name (currently "Sofi").
- Update your Login Email.
- Change your Password.

## 4. Payment & Order Flow

Your checkout process is designed to be smooth and reliable for your customers:

- **Razorpay Integration:** Customers can pay securely using UPI, Credit/Debit Cards, or Net Banking via the Razorpay gateway.
- **WhatsApp Integration:** For customers who prefer a personal touch, they can complete their order via WhatsApp, sending you a formatted message with their order details.

## 5. Technical Details (For Your Records)

Your website is built on a modern, high-performance "MERN" technology stack to ensure it is fast, secure, and ready to scale as your business grows.

- **Frontend:** Built with React.js and Tailwind CSS for a beautiful, mobile-friendly design.
- **Backend:** Powered by Node.js and Express.js.
- **Database:** MongoDB (Your data is stored securely in the cloud).
- **Image Hosting:** Cloudinary (Ensures your product photos load quickly without slowing down the website).
- **SEO Ready:** Optimized with Meta Tags and Open Graph data so your website looks great when shared on WhatsApp, Facebook, or Instagram.
